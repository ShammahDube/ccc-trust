Who We Are

Community Compassion Care Trust is a local initiative for vulnerable young people, women and men who struggle to fulfill their dreams because of a never ending cycle of poverty.

The organisation seeks to provide quality and effective community service to young people, vulnerable groups and communities to improve their livelihood through initiating income gerating projects, life skills development, promote quality of behaviour change, communication in areas of HIV/AIDS and sexual reproductive health rights at community level.

The organisation also seeks to promote access to education through formal and non-formal education for the empowerment of young people and to promote access to sustainable safe water and environment sanitation through effective water resource management in vulnerable communities through a well- coordinated multi-sectoral approach.

It is the organisations desire to end crime rate in the local communities through community engagement activities and programs.

What Caused Us to Form the Trust

We were driven by a great passion to empower the communities to better their livelihood and stop crimes, divorces and child marriages. We thank God for this.

Objectives

  • 1. To promote access to sustainable safe water and environmental sanitation through effective water resource management in vulnerable communities
  • 2. To promote quality of life through the promotion of behavior change communication in areas of HIV/AIDS and sexual reproductive health rights at community level.
  • 3. To capacitate and empower young people, women and men economically and skillfully so as to improve their livelihoods.
  • 4. To promote access to education through formal and informal education for the empowerment of young people.
  • 5. To effectively respond and equip communities in disaster risk management.

Values

Transperancy, Tolerance, uBuntu, Innovation, Empowerment, Unity and Peace.

Vision

An empowered Community in all spheres of life.

Mission

To deliver through our services a Zimbabwe free of poverty, hunger, disease where all life can thrive, free of fear and violence, promoting high literacy rate, through equitable and access to quality education at all levels, to health care and social protection, where physical, mental and social well-being are assured

What We Do

These are the services we provide to the community:

Online Lessons

We offer FREE online lessons for O-level and A-level students.

Skill-building

We empower men, women & youth with FREE skills to better their lives, e.g manufacturing bead bags and purses.

Environmetal Awareness

We provide FREE environmetal management education and clean up campaigns with other organisations.

Counselling

We provide FREE 'SRHR', 'GENDA' & counselling online.

Refferals

We refer people to the relevant authorities for help.

Promoting Impartiality

We unite people and ensure they tolerate each other despite race, religion, party affiliation etc.

Community Advice

We advise the community on health and social issues such as preserving & conserving water and not drinking borehole water before boiling it.

COVID-19

We run COVID-19 awareness campaigns at bus stops and bank queues. We sanitise public areas and donate PPE to the public.
